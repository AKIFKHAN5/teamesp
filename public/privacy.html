Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
Terms Privacy Policy Refund Policy Disclaimer

Overview

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect when you use Team ESP, how it is used, and the choices you have. We collect the minimum information necessary to process your order and provide support — nothing more.

Information We Collect

  • Order details: your name, WhatsApp/Telegram contact number, and selected subscription plan — needed to process and deliver your order.
  • Payment confirmation: screenshots or transaction references you send us manually via WhatsApp/Telegram to verify payment. We do not store full card or banking credentials.
  • Basic analytics: general website traffic data (pages visited, browser type) used only to improve site performance.
  • Reviews: the name and comment you submit when leaving a review, displayed publicly on our site.

What We Never Ask For

We will never ask for your PUBG Mobile account password, email password, banking PIN, or OTP codes. If anyone contacts you claiming to be Team ESP and requests these details, do not share them — report it to us immediately.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and activate your subscription order.
  • To contact you regarding your order status or support requests.
  • To improve our website and service based on aggregated, anonymous usage patterns.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal local browser storage (not tracking cookies) to remember your cart, wishlist, and theme preference between visits. This data stays in your browser and is not sent to any third party.

Your Rights

You may request a copy of the information we hold about your orders, or request its deletion, at any time by contacting our support team via WhatsApp or Telegram.
